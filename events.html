Santa Margarita River Hike

October 28, 2018 | 9 AM–NOON

The Santa Margarita River is one of the last free-flowing rivers in San Diego County. The river peacefully flows under a canopy of tall trees, between banks lined with dense riparian growth. This is a cool, refreshing place for a hike any time of the year. Wildlife, including deer and other large mammals, depend on this river corridor. The area is also noted for raptors that may be seen overhead or perched in trees. This is an intermediate 3 mile hike with an elevation gain/loss of up to 500 feet.
